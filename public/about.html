About the project

Cloud-powered support for better study sessions

Learnify is a cloud-based AI study assistant that helps students turn notes into summaries, key points and quiz questions.

AI

How It Helps

Students paste lecture notes into the study workspace. The application organizes the material into a concise summary, important points and review questions that can support revision.

Study workflow

How Learnify Works

From lecture notes to study material in a few simple steps.

1

Paste Notes

User enters or pastes lecture notes into the study page.

2

AI Analyzes Content

AI reads the notes and identifies important information.

3

Generate Summary

The app creates a short and clear summary of the notes.

4

Create Quiz Questions

The app generates practice questions to help students review.

5

Save Study Session

The notes and AI results can be saved to history for later review.

Use AI thoughtfully

Responsible AI

AI is a study aid, not a replacement for course material, instructors or student judgment.

  • 1
    AI results may not always be perfect

    Generated content can include mistakes or miss important context.

  • 2
    Review summaries and quiz questions

    Compare every result with your original notes and trusted course resources.

  • 3
    Protect private information

    Do not enter personal, private, confidential or sensitive information.

  • 4
    Human review is important

    Students remain responsible for deciding whether generated material is accurate and useful.
